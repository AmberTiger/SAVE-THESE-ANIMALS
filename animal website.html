African Wild Dogs

These dogs are hunted by humans and larger predators, and killed by diseases. They are also dying because of habit loss and competition with larger animals. There are only around 6,600 of them left.

Black Footed Ferrets

These ferrets are dying because the shortgrass prairie habitat they depend on are plowed for crops. Also, the prairie dogs they eat are dying, reducing the ferrets' food source. There are only around 300 of them left.
